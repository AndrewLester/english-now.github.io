English for Health Care

Improve your English for use in a health care, public health, or medical research setting. Whether you are a clinician, policymaker, or a researcher, your studies with English Now! will help you develop the communication skills you need. English Now! serves many students from the National Institutes of Health, PAHO, and other health care settings. We are familiar with the needs of students that work in health care or engage in medical research. Ask us about a curriculum that aligns with your needs.

English for Health Care Curriculum

The needs of students pursuing careers in health care or public health are varied. Students may have to complete NCLEX or TOEFL requirements, or medical boards, to practice in the United States, or they may be thinking about presenting the results of their research to their peers, in English. We have been delighted to work with a wide range of medical/health care practitioners from around the world, and it brings us great joy to know that their improved communication skills may enable them to serve others more effectively.

Whatever your area of interest, ask us about a curriculum and approach to lessons that has the greatest impact on your use of English language. We will help you select a curriculum that is targeted to your needs, and we can also help you with general English classes that improve your comprehension, accurate use of grammar, and pronunciation – even extending beyond your English studies for your profession.

If you are interested in English for Health Care programs, we would be happy to test your English proficiency and recommend a program tied to the competencies you are developing. We are also glad to talk to administrators in a lab or hospital setting, if that would be of assistance.

Contact Us

If you have questions, or to learn more on how to enroll in the English Now! Business Sales or Business English Programs, please visit us at the school or contact us.

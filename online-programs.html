English Now! Online: Meeting You Where You Are

Our Personalized Approach Goes Beyond the Classroom

English Now! Online is an option for students who want to extend their classroom experience from the convenience of their home or office. Your 45-minute private online tutorial will be engaging and interactive. Online tutorials emphasize the skills that are most important to your studies: conversational English, grammar, reading, writing, listening, or speaking. English Now! Online is a great option for busy parents and professionals who want a flexible way to improve their English. These sessions offer the same unique and personalized approach that makes English Now! a special place to learn English.

What You Will Need

To take your online classes, you will need (1) a desktop or laptop computer with a camera, tablet, or even a smartphone; (2) a microphone; and (3) high-speed internet access.

Schedule and Tuition

  • You will schedule a weekly online meeting at a convenient time for you. Online lessons are 45 minutes long, and tuition is $40/session. Students must enroll for a minimum of 10 online sessions.
  • New students will also pay a one-time $40 registration fee. Books and materials, if required, are provided and billed at the cost to the school.

Questions and How to Enroll

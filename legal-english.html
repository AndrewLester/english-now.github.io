English in Law & Diplomacy

Whether you are engaged in commercial or public international legal practice, you understand the value of succinct, accurate English. Attorneys everywhere appreciate well-written English, and the ability to speak persuasively is prized in the law.

English Now! likewise appreciates these values, and we look forward to supporting legal practitioners from around the world as they refine their use of English. Our instructors are specifically trained to refine your ability to argue and write effectively; to represent your organization, company, or country well; and to achieve your goals.

Legal English studies at English Now! are undertaken through private lessons that are tailored to the unique needs of the student. Given that legal practitioners’ needs are so varied, we believe that private lessons are the most efficient and effective manner to serve students in this area. Please note that our Legal English instructors have earned both a J.D. degree and a TEFL certification, making this program particularly effective. In addition, English Now! draws on extensive Legal English curricula as we craft a private lesson program to meet your needs.

Given the special approach that we take to Legal English, tuition for Legal English private lessons is $50 per hour for classes taught at English Now! and $60 per hour for classes taught offsite (offices, embassies, etc.). In addition, there is a one-time $40 registration fee for all new students. Please note that these tuition rates are somewhat higher than our customary private lesson tuition rate, consistent with the qualifications of our instructors and the special approach we are taking to this curriculum area.

Please contact us if you have any questions about Legal English at English Now!, and we look forward to the opportunity to help you improve your legal English!

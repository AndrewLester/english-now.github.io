Student Services

Our staff is always available to help you during your stay with us at English Now! In addition to the quality language classes you will receive at our school, we also offer a variety of student services to make your stay more enjoyable:

In office, mail, and phone support: We welcome you to stop in and visit with us to discuss what English Now! can do for you. Our students are from all over the world – 89 different countries of origin in the last three years alone – so we are asked all kinds of questions and are always willing to help or – if we can’t – to try to help you find someone who can. Our staff is available in the office or by phone or email to answer any questions you have about our programs, the school, and life in the United States in general.

Welcome materials: When you start classes, you will receive a packet with useful information about the school, the Washington, DC metro region and life in the United States in general. We also have some practical suggestions about finding opportunities in the area to practice your language skills. Whether you have just arrived in the United States or you have been here for years, we look forward to welcoming you to the area.

Extracurricular activities: During your stay, we encourage you to take advantage of English Now! activities. Please visit our activities page to learn more about the events coming up in the next few months.

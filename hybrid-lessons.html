Hybrid Lessons: Meeting You Wherever You Are

Our Personalized Approach Goes Beyond the Classroom

  • A "Hybrid" program is a combination of face-to-face learning and online studies. Hybrid programs arefor English Now! students who would like the flexibility to meet with their teacher online from home or any convenient location.
  • This program enhances your classroom instruction with online tutorials that emphasize whatever is most important to your studies: conversational English, grammar, reading, writing, listening, or speaking.
  • Our online sessions offer the same unique and personalized approach that makes English Now! a special place to learn English.

Schedule

You will schedule a weekly onlinemeeting time that works well for your schedule. If you are already enrolled in our Monday-Thursday classes, you might want to schedule your online lesson on a Friday. If you are an alumni of our school, you might want to schedule your weekly online meeting during your lunch. There are many options!

What You Will Need

  • Your private online tutorial will be engaging and interactive, just like your classroom lessons! Each online lesson is a 45-minute tutorial with your teacher.
  • To take your online classes, you will need (1) a desktop or laptop computer with a camera, tablet, or even a smartphone; (2) a microphone; and (3) high-speed internet access.
  • Our staff will provide training so thatit will be easy tomeet your teacher online. We will also check in with you regularly about your progress.

Hybrid Learning Options

Conversation Plus Hybrid - Want more instruction on specific skills? Enhance your conversation class with one or more private sessions/week.

Intensive Plus Hybrid - Need homework help? Enhance your Intensive English class with one or more private online lessons/week.

Special Interest Hybrid Lessons - Want to work on your presentation skills or business communications? Review grammar skills, practice new vocabulary, improve your pronunciation, and more.

Tuition

There are several hybrid-learning options available, whether you are currently enrolled or an English Now! alumni. Introductory pricing for enrollment by June 30:

Conversation Plus Hybrid - ($120/4 online tutorials with Conversation class enrolllment)

Intensive Plus Hybrid - ($120/4 online tutorials with Intensive class enrollment)

Special Interest Hybrid Lessons - ($240/8 online tutorials; private lessons at English Now! or an off-site location priced separately)

Questions and How to Enroll

For more information about our hybrid English language programs or to register for a free trial session, please contact us.

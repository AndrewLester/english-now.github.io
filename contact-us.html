Phone, Email or Visit

Whether you are in the Washington, D.C. area, or halfway around the world, we look forward to hearing from you.

If you would like to visit the school, our office is open from 9:00am to 5:00pm Monday through Thursday, 9:00am until 1:00pm on Friday, and 9:30am until 12:00pm on Saturday. We are also open in the evenings, from 6:00pm until 7:30pm, on Tuesday and Thursday.

Please feel free to call, visit, or email us at any time with your questions, and we will respond as soon as possible.

A Special Place to Learn English.

Serving the Washington, DC area international community since 1991, English Now! offers English language courses for students of all levels. It brings us great joy to welcome you to our school. Come see our school through our welcome video!

We take a distinctive approach to English language study. To ensure that each student benefits from personalized instruction, class sizes are small. Our group classes enroll a maximum of six students, and many classes are even smaller.

We offer a warm, welcoming atmosphere to a wonderfully diverse set of students from around the world. In the past seven years, over 4000 students from 95 different countries have studied with us! We hope that all of our students feel at home during their time at English Now!

Whether you are a resident of the Washington, DC area or a visitor, we invite you to learn “English for life” at English Now! To learn about great ESL classes, keep in touch about our school activities or to stay in touch with your classmates or friends, please visit us at the school, follow us on Facebook, or contact us. Please also visit us at ENcompass, where we provide useful information and ideas online, to our friends and family around the world!

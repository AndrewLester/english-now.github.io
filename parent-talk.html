Parent Talk

Parent Talk is an informal conversation group for parents that meets on Tuesdays, from 1:30-2:30pm, and on Wednesdays and Fridays from 10:15-11:15am. Children from newborn to three years old are welcome. Each week we focus on a specific parenting theme, such as feeding, potty training, role of fathers, self-care, discipline, attachment, sleeping, etc. We also often study English language phrasal verbs that relate to parenting. Sometimes short readings are used to guide the discussion on topics that we identify, as a group.

Please join us to talk about English, culture, and parenting, and we look forward to meeting you!

Parent Talk is led by Elizabeth Boesen. More information about Elizabeth here.

English Classes for Youth

English Now! offers English programs for children. Our programs enroll children from dozens of countries all over the world. We especially enjoy helping children of newly-arrived international residents of the Washington, D.C. area prepare for and succeed in school. We look forward to serving your family!

English Now! offers the following English classes for children and teens:

Afternoon Summer Youth Program, for Children Ages Eight to Twelve

  • Our Summer Youth Program meets four days per week, on Mondays through Thursdays, from 2:30 until 4:30 pm. These classes are taught as semi-private lessons with a formal curriculum. Classes also include significant experiential learning (music, drama, art, activities in the neighborhood, etc.).
  • This program is available from late June through August, each year.
  • Students are invited to participate in our regular Friday activities (visits to Washington, DC-area museums, monuments, etc.). A family member must accompany students 12 and under to activities.
  • Each semi-private lesson enrolls two to four students.
  • Tuition is $200 per week. (Note: If only one student is enrolled at a given level of English proficiency, the class will be taught as a 90-minute private lesson, but tuition will remain the same. If the class size reaches four students, the class will be extended to two and a half hours at no additional charge.)
  • There is a one-time $40 registration fee. For classes that use textbooks, there is a charge of $40 for books & materials.
  • Minimum registration is for two weeks of classes.

After School or Weekend Private Lessons, for Children Ages Five to Twelve

  • These classes are tailored to the needs of the family and the student(s). Lessons are most often in a private or semi-private format. Our instructors are able to structure lessons to best match the needs of the student as well as the student's daytime school curriculum.
  • During the summer, private lessons for children and youth meet twice a week, either Monday and Wednesday from 4:30 until 5:30pm, OR Tuesday and Thursday from 4:30 to 5:30pm. We may suggest a longer lesson for older students. Some instructors may also be available for private lessons on Fridays or Saturdays. Ask us when instructors are available.
  • Between September and June, private lessons for children and youth are usually held in the afternoons or on Saturday morning. Ask us when instructors are available.
  • Students are invited to participate in our regular Friday activities (visits to Washington, DC-area museums, monuments, etc.). A family member must accompany students 12 and under to activities.
  • Tuition for private lessons is $40 per hour for classes at the school. If an instructor is available to travel to a family's home, private lessons may sometimes be possible offsite for tuition of $50 per hour. In addition, there is a one-time $40 registration fee, and a $40 fee for books & materials in classes that use textbooks.
  • Minimum registration is for 10 hours of lessons.

Intensive English Program for Youth Age 13 and Up

  • During January, February, and the summer months, English Now!'s Intensive English Program enrolls groups of teenagers, age 13 and up. This is a great program with a maximum of six students per class.
  • For more information regarding our Intensive English Program, including term dates, click here.
  • During other months, older teenagers may be able to study in our Intensive English Program, depending on their age and level of English proficiency.
  • In addition, teenaged students may enroll in private lessons, year-round. Ask us when instructors are available. For more information regarding private lessons, click here. We also have special programs for teens that focus on writing, an especially important skill area for students that want to study at the university level in the U.S. For information about our writing for teens classes, see here. Please contact us with questions regarding youth programs. We look forward to meeting you and your family!

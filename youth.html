Teaching Youth at English Now!

Teaching English as a second language to youth is very different than teaching to adult learners. Each group has specific learning needs and styles, so English Now! has a separate curriculum for young people that reflects these differences.

Summer Youth Program 2015 English Classes for Children and Teens

English Now! offers afternoon English programs for children and teenagers during July and August. These programs are taught through semi-private classroom English lessons as well as outdoor activities.

Our 2008-2014 summer programs for teenagers enrolled students from dozens of countries in Asia, Europe, and Latin America, as well as international residents of the Washington, DC area. We look forward to meeting even more students and their families in 2015!

Based on our experience, English Now! recommends the following approach to summer English classes for children and teens:

  • Teenagers that study with us for three or more weeks in July or August may enroll in a class for teens in our Intensive English Program.
  • Classes for younger students, and for teenagers here for fewer than three weeks during late June, July or August, meet four days per week, on Mondays through Thursdays, from 2:30 until 4:30 pm.  
  • Students are invited to participate in our regular Friday activities (visits to Washington, DC-area museums, monuments, etc.).
    •   
  • Classes in the afternoons are taught as semi-private lessons with a formal curriculum. Classes also include significant experiential learning (music, drama, art, activities in the neighborhood, etc.).  
  • Each semi-private lesson enrolls two to four students.  
  • Tuition is $200 per week. (Note: If only one student is enrolled at a given level of English proficiency, the class will be taught as a 90-minute private lesson, but tuition will remain the same. If the class size reaches four students, the class will be extended to two and a half hours at no additional charge.)  
  • In addition, there may be a charge of $30 for books & materials, and there is a one-time $40 registration fee.  
  • Minimum registration is for two weeks of classes. 

English Now! may be able to arrange classes for children and youth on other schedules. Contact us for more information. Summer classes for children and youth in 2015 start on Monday, June 15.

For more information on Summer 2015 programs for children and youth, which will bring together students from across the world, please contact us to request information. We look forward to serving you this summer!

ESOL Tutorials for Youth & Teenagers

English Now! serves international families resident in the Washington, DC area with children that are in need of supplemental ESOL.

Our instructors are available for lessons in the home or at the school. Curriculum is tailored to the needs of the family and the student(s). Lessons are most often in a private or semi-private format. Our instructors are able to structure lessons to best match the needs of the student as well as the student’s daytime curriculum.

Please note that the cost of our English lessons for youth at our school is generally far lower than the cost of in-home tutorials in the Bethesda/Silver Springs/Potomac Rockville areas. In addition, our instructors are certified and experienced working with children and teens. (Please ask us about our approach to working with children and youth.) We look forward to meeting your family.

 

Please contact us with questions regarding youth programs. We look forward to meeting you and your family!

English Classes for Youth

English Now! offers English programs for children. Our programs enroll children from dozens of countries all over the world. We especially enjoy helping children of newly-arrived international residents of the Washington, D.C. area prepare for and succeed in school. We look forward to serving your family!

English Now! offers the following English classes for children and teens:

After School or Weekend Private Lessons, for Children Ages Five to Twelve

  • Purpose - These classes are tailored to the needs of the family and the student(s). Lessons are most often in a private or semi-private format. Our instructors are able to structure lessons to match the needs of the student as well as the student's daytime school curriculum.
  • Relationship to Children's Studies in the School - We are fortunate to have many good ESOL programs in the schools in this area. However, we know that tutorial support will accelerate a student's path to proficiency and—especially for students that hesitate to speak out—help their oral proficiency and active vocabulary progress more quickly.
  • Curriculum - We have a number of textbook and curriculum options available to support these lessons. Ask us for information about curriculum—we are glad to share our recommendations, based on work with many students over the years.
  • Dates - Between September and June, private and semi-private lessons for children and youth typically meet in the afternoons after school at 3:00pm, 4:00pm or 5:00pm or on Saturday morning.
  • Schedule - We schedule 10 minutes between lessons, for instructors and students to switch classes. That means that a one-hour tutorial lesson is held for 50 minutes. Some older students may take a one and a half hour lesson, which is scheduled for 80 minutes. Ask us when instructors are available. For lower level students (students in ESOL 1 and most in ESOL 2 classes in their school), we recommend private lessons at least two times per week, usually on Monday and Wednesday, or Tuesday and Thursday.
  • Tuition - Tuition for private lessons is $40 per 50-minute session, for classes at the school. If an instructor is available to travel to a family's home, offsite private lessons may sometimes be possible offsite for tuition of $50 per hour, but availability for these lessons is very limited, and lessons are scheduled at students' homes for a minimum of one hour and 15 minutes. Tuition for semi-private lessons of two students is $28 per 50-minute session per student at the school, and $38 per hour per student offsite.
  • Fees - In addition, there is a one-time $40 registration fee, and an additional charge for any books & materials.
  • Registration - Lessons for youth are scheduled for the entire Fall or Spring Semesters.

After School or Weekend Private Lessons, for Teens

  • Purpose - These classes match the needs of the student as well as the student's daytime school curriculum.
  • Curriculum - Curriculum is as varied as the needs of teenaged students in public and private schools, and we are glad to tailor lessons to the academic support, vocabulary development, and other needs of students. We also have special programs for teens that focus on writing, an especially important skill area for students that want to study at the university level in the U.S. For information about our Writing for Teens classes, see here.
  • Dates - Between September and June, private and semi-private lessons for children and youth typically meet in the afternoons after school at 3:00pm, 4:00pm or 5:00pm or on Saturday morning.
  • Schedule - We schedule 10 minutes between lessons, for instructors and students to switch classes. That means that a one-hour tutorial lesson is held for 50 minutes, or a one and a half hour lesson is scheduled for 80 minutes.
  • Tuition - Tuition for private lessons for teens is $40 per 50-minute session and $60 per 80-minute session, for classes at the school. Tuition for semi-private lessons of two students is $28 per 50- minute session per student at the school, and $38 per hour per student offsite.
  • Fees - In addition, there is a one-time $40 registration fee, and an additional charge for any books & materials.
  • Registration - Lessons for teens are scheduled for the entire Fall or Spring Semesters.

Intensive English Program for Youth Age 13 and Up

  • Purpose - These classes are great for families visiting relatives in the area in the, and especially for international families that want their children to make progress in English during the summer, before school starts.
  • Calendar - During January, February, and the summer months, English Now!'s Intensive English Program enrolls groups of teenagers, age 13 and up. This is a great program with a maximum of six students per class. During other months, older teenagers may be able to study in our Intensive English Program, depending on their age and level of English proficiency.
  • Program - For more information regarding our Intensive English Program, including term dates, tuition, and fees, click here.

Afternoon Summer Youth Program, for Children Ages Eight to Twelve

  • Purpose - These classes are great for families visiting relatives in the area in the summer, and especially for international families that want their children to make progress in English during the summer, before school starts.
  • Schedule - Our Summer Youth Program meets four days per week, on Mondays through Thursdays, from 2:30 until 4:30 pm.
  • Curriculum - These classes follow a formal curriculum and enroll two to five students. Classes also include significant experiential learning (music, drama, art, activities in the neighborhood, etc.).
  • Dates - This program is available from late June through August, each year.
  • Friday Outings - Students are invited to participate in our regular Friday activities (visits to Washington, DC-area museums, monuments, etc.). A family member must accompany all students 12 and under to activities.
  • Tuition - Tuition is $200 per week. (Note: If only one student is enrolled at a given level of English proficiency, the class will be taught as a 90-minute private lesson, but tuition will remain the same.)
  • Fees - There is a one-time $40 registration fee. For classes that use textbooks, there is an additional charge for books & materials.
  • Registration - Minimum registration is for two weeks of classes.

Please contact us with questions regarding youth programs. We look forward to meeting you and your family!

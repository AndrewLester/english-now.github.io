Small and Varied Classes

We invite you to “learn English for life” at English Now! If you are thinking about additional English language studies, you may want to consider the following:

  • Class Size – Our group classes are small, enrolling a maximum of six students!
  • Personalized Instruction – With small classes, our teachers are able to actively design curriculum to suit the needs of students.
  • Welcoming Atmosphere – Learn in a warm and supportive environment. Visit our school and see just how distinctive our atmosphere is.
  • Great Location – We are located only a short walk from downtown Bethesda and the Bethesda metro.
  • Student Body – Our student body is remarkably international. Over 2500 students from 95 countries have studied with us in the last five years alone!

Whether you’re interested in our Intensive English Program, seminars, conversation classes, private lessons, or a TOEFL preparation class, English Now!’s commitment to personalized instruction and student service make it a great place to study English.

For more information about English Now! and our unique approach to English language study, please visit, call or email the school today.
